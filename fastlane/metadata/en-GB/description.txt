WaySplit is a travel expense splitting tool designed to track every expense, automatically split costs, and clearly show who owes what when the trip is over.

Key Features:
- Create trips with destination, dates, and base currency
- Snap a photo or pick receipts from your album — AI auto-detects amounts, merchants, and items
- Multi-currency support with exchange rate conversion
- Custom split groups with flexible cost-sharing options per expense
- Auto-calculated settlements with minimal transfer suggestions
- Spending breakdown by category (dining, transport, shopping, accommodation, and more)
- Manual entry and quick actions supported
